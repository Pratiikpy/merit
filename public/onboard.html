Get paid when AI cites you.

Paste your RSS feed. Merit turns your work into a citable source and pays you in USDC every time an agent grounds an answer in it — only when the citation passes verification.

No account, no key. The wallet is receive-only. To direct payouts to your own wallet, add merit-verify:0xYourAddress anywhere in your feed.
  1. Paste your feed — Merit reads your recent posts and registers you as a creator with an on-chain (ERC-8004) identity.
  2. Agents cite you — when a Merit agent uses your work to answer a question, your citation goes through proof-of-citation verification.
  3. You earn — every verified citation settles sub-cent USDC to your wallet on Arc. Hallucinated or unsupported citations pay nothing.
Merit — a proof-of-work economy for AI agents on Arc. Verified citations only.
